The Process

How It Works

Hover over a step to learn more

1. Profile Setup

Get started in under 60 seconds.

Set up your child’s profile, incl. subject gaps and strengths

2. Ability Check

Identifying current skill levels.

Our quick start assessment subtly checks for foundational gaps without causing testing anxiety.

3. Individual Path

Custom curriculum generation.

View conclusions and proposed learning paths. A unique learning map is generated, prioritising what your child needs to master next.

4. Daily Exercise

Engaging bite-sized exercises.

Child starts with ability-adapted exercises. Short 10 to 15 minute runs that feel like progress, not like homework.

5. Progress Diagnostic

Progress & obstacles are identified

Improvement is tracked, progress is measured constantly. Alerts or weekly reports show exactly where they are excelling and where they might need more time.

6. Path Adaption

Taking the optimal path

The learning path adapts to performance and progress. And if required provides additional hints and lessons, never just the answers.

7. Mastery

Proficiency and grades improves

After 4 to 7 weeks measurable improvement materialises. From there the learning path optimises to the child.

Frequently Asked Questions

What subjects does Smartmeister cover?

expand_more
Currently, we focus on core Math and Language skills (primarily German). We will be rapidly expanding into Science, Music/Arts and maybe Coding/Programming modules soon.

Is the curriculum aligned with school standards?

expand_more
Yes, our content is designed to complement major international and local curricula, ensuring the skills learned are directly applicable to your child's school performance.

How long should a child use it daily?

expand_more
We recommend short, high-impact sessions of 10–15 minutes. This prevents digital fatigue while maximizing long-term knowledge retention.

expand_more
Data safety is our top priority. We are 100% GDPR compliant, and your child's learning data never leaves our secure servers or is sold to third parties.
