Our VISION

Learning struggles are not a choice. They are often caused by intrinsic gaps in foundational knowledge or because children are non-typical learners

Better learning isn’t just about putting more content or learning materials in front of children. It’s about providing the right tools with tailored challenges at the right pace. Primary pupils that grapple with basics like multiplication, fractions, meaning of vocabulary or grammar do so when the learning curve is too steep or too flat, when learning-patterns differ or when emotional anxiety has built around their inabilities.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is this platform about? Why did we start Smartmeister?

We originally started Smartmeister as an e-learning platform to support primary school children from multilingual backgrounds to better learn school curriculum subjects. Regardless of language-related learning challenges. Learning German often takes much longer than the support period provided by additional language classes, hence for these kids mastering the curriculum stays a challenge. Having to learn subjects in the German curriculum is hard if one’s parents do not speak German at home. Unfamiliarity with important concepts, semantics and foundational knowledge due to cultural origins or upbringing prove to be further obstacles. This is where Smartmeister fills the gap: Empowering pupils to better understand and learn curriculum subjects despite language barriers.

Who is it for? Is Smartmeister helpful for all primary school students?

Besides of language barriers, many comprehension difficulties are also rooted in foundational knowledge gaps or intrinsic deficiencies. To that end Smartmeister not just helps students step-by-step to tackle their weakness (“…fill these gaps!”) in an adaptively challenging yet engaging learning journey, but also supports pupils…

Catch up on curriculum subjects

Strengthen existing subject knowledge

Deepen knowledge

Prepare for test assessments and homework

How does it help children learn better?

We propel better learning with practical on-platform tools such as text-to-voice pronunciation, additional exercise explanations (plus: even simpler rephrasing of tasks) and individually aligned exercises that align difficulty level and complexity to the child’s abilities.  

For multilingual children Smartmeister provides granular translation tools, entailing word-by-word or partial-sentence translations, also offering voice output in their respective language + German in order to facilitate intrinsic language learning while training on curriculum subjects.

Why does personalised learning with exercises that adapt to student’s abilities help improve learning?

There is a fine balance between exercises too easy and too hard. In order to effectively learn children need the certainty of a predictable learning environment by allowing for consistent, manageable challenges and providing clear, immediate feedback.

‍

How’s our approach better or different than other learning platforms?

Other platforms do not involve exercises that individually adapt their difficulty level to the student’s abilities. We also plan to better balance gamification (incl. granular options), avoiding some of the undesirable behavioural patterns that occur in strongly gamified platforms that offer addictive reward systems (collecting coins for games).

Our key ambition is to provide an intuitive learning interface that meaningfully brings all “comprehension-assisting” tools into one place, without having to use complicated work-arounds, thus creating optimal engagement, intrinsic growth, and a sense of accomplishment.

Why are you focusing on primary school students first?

Based on personal experience of Smartmeister’s founders and frequent learning+teaching issues reported in primary schools across Germany, but also considering the greater number of tuition offerings for secondary school students, the team decided helping primary pupils first, building an integrated learning platform that focuses on better subject understanding.

What subjects are provided?

We want to focus on the first 6 years of primary + early secondary school. For its initial launch Smartmeister will provide exercises + materials for year 3 and 4 in Maths and German, which will be quickly expanded to other years and subjects.

Here is the list of the subjects that are planned:

  • Year 1: Maths, German, General Studies + Ethics, English,
  • Year 2: Maths, German, General Studies + Ethics, English
  • Year 3: Maths, German, General Studies + Ethics, English
  • Year 4: Maths, German, General Studies + Ethics, English
  • Year 5: Maths, German, Science (incl. Social Science), English
  • Year 6: Maths, German, Science (incl. Social Science), English

Smartmeister may expand to grade level 7 to 12 secondary school curriculums at a later time.

How long do children need to train daily?

Already 15 to 25 minutes daily doing a series of exercises make a measurable difference, either improving or reinforcing learned subjects. Students can also listening comprehension and reading fluency with targeted materials and step-by-step video explanations.

Can I signup for early access? When Does the product launch?

We have not planned early access yet, but we will start a test phase soon, in which we assess practical use of comprehension-focused functionality and on-platform learning.  The platform launch is planned in 2026.

How is the learning content created?

Our team creates, curates and validates every exercise, ensuring that curriculum standards are followed and pedagogical principles for effective learning are upheld. In-house subject and content guidelines help the team to maintain quality standards across all subjects.

How do we protect children’s information?

Strictly following GDPR-regulations personal data is exclusively stored on EU-servers. We do not use third-party providers outside the EU for processing such data. Providing a safe and secure environment for children is one of our top priorities, including the data of children and parents!
