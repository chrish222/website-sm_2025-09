Learning paths that grow with them

A learning journey that only moves on when students stand on solid foundations

There’s no rushing ahead before German or Math subject topics are truly understood. The learning path adjusts to your child’s actual performance, reinforcing what needs more time, unlocking what’s next when they’re ready.

The right challenge level makes all the difference to atypical learners

Smartmeister is judgement-free and enables kids to practice at their own pace, unlike in stressed one or bi-weekly Math & German tutoring sessions. Exercises, hard enough to be interesting, achievable enough to feel good. The app monitors when one gets repeatedly stuck, adjusting the route before frustration sets in.

Every child can build confidence — including those who find it harder

Various levels of dyslexia, dyscalculia and attention challenges are more common than many think. Smartmeister supports these learners too — with dyslexia-friendly typefaces, text-to-speech read-aloud, and display settings that reduce clutter so nothing gets in the way of understanding.
